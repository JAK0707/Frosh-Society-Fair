SOCIETY FAIR

Society Fair, an eagerly anticipated event hosted by FROSH at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, offers a vibrant platform for newly admitted students to explore the diverse array of societies on campus. With stalls showcasing the myriad opportunities available, students can engage with different groups, learn about their activities, and even join by filling recruitment forms.
